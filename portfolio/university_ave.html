New University Avenue

Final project for a 3D Modeling, Visualization and Simulation course at the University of Florida during the fall of 2017.

I re-designed University Avenue in Gainesville, FL to reflect the newly adopted land use changes and how those relate to the existing conditions. The project was based on Alachua County parcel and building footprint data, utilized and manipulated through ArcGIS Pro. I coded new CGA rules in CityEngine to extrude old and new buildings in 3D, and used existing CGA rules for Complete Streets and Parks. I added a bicycle lane replacing the existing center turn lane inspired by the bicycle lane in Washington, DC. I extruded some existing buildings using SketchUp.
