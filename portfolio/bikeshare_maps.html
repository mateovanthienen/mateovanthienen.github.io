Bikeshare Maps

I created these visualizations to show how different bikeshare systems perform. I accessed the data from each system's respective open data portal.

Graduated points show how much each station is used in relation to other stations in the system and graduated lines show the most popular trips in between stations. Bikes in these systems are not GPS-enabled so we cannot see where specifically each bike traveled between the origin and the destination. These visualizations, however, help paint a picture of travel patterns such as last-mile solutions to transit, recreational use around parks, and changes in use dependent on bicycle infrastructure quality.

These Carto maps are interactive so feel free to zoom in and out and to click on the points to find out station names and their respective use:
